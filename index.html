Instructions
A poor Balloon Seller has NO money to buy Air Cylinders for his Balloon Shop! He got a billionare offer to sell Balloons for a Birthday Party!! Help him by collecting Air Packets on the Screen in return for Half of his profits!!

Click on the Air Packets to destroy the Air Packets
Smaller the Air Packet Higher the point
When 75% of Screen is filled you have 10 seconds to Clear the Screen
When the Timer Runs out -
GAME OVER


Power Ups

Use "Felix Felices" to pause the screen for 5 seconds and you can clear the screen! Remember you get only 2 of them!


Use "Infinity Gauntlet" to destroy Half of the Air Packet Population in one Snap! But you get them Only at difficult times Use Wisely!


Rock Air Packets are made with Platinum Core!! They are Harder to Break! But you get the costliest Noble Gas inside it! You get 10 times the Score! and Tell you the Number of Clicks Required!
